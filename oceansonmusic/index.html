Dylan Stephenson

Ocean Son Creator and Lead Producer

No More--

Link to Song
No More is the first song I wrote and fully produced through Ocean Son. A few months after going through a difficult breakup, I had the realization that I was happier, healthier, and more confident than I had been during the relationship which lead to me writing this song. I finished the song after meeting my now wife, Ashlie. She inspired the "She's everything" moment at the beginning and end of the song. ×

Heartbeat--

Link to Song
I wrote Heartbeat a few months after my wife and I got married. I started feeling a lot of pressure and anxiety knowing that all of my life choices would start directly affecting her. The Peter Gabriel-esque chord progression and the somber melody capture that feeling of worry and anxiety. The tonal shift in the chorus gives the feeling that even though hearing my wife's heartbeat makes me feel that pressure, having her with me is worth it. ×

Fallin' Apart--

Link to Song
Fallin' Apart was not only co-produced but also co-written alongside my brother, Beau Stephenson. We wanted to write something a little more funky than any of our previous songs. Fallin' Apart is not meant to have a deep message or poetic lyrics. Our goal writing this song was to find a good groove between the piano, bass, guitar, and drums. Syncopating the vocals along with all of the other elements makes this song incredibly catchy and fun to listen to every time. ×
